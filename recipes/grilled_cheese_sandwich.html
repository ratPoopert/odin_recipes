Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Bread, butter, and Cheddar cheese; here's a way to make this classic sandwich in a nonstick pan.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices White Bread
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Butter, divided
  • 1 slice Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
  2. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  3. Place bread in skillet with buttered side down. Add 1 slice of cheese to the bread.
  4. Butter a second slice of bread on one side. With the buttered side up, place the second slice of bread on top of the sandwich.
  5. Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted.
