Scrambled Eggs

Description

Follow these simple steps and recipe on how to make the absolute best scambled eggs.

Follow these simple steps and recipe on how to make the absolute best scambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Half & Half
  • 1/4 teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 1 tablespoon Unsalted Butter
  • Black Pepper, for serving
  • Freshly chopped Herbs, for serving

Steps

  1. In a medium mixing bowl, aggressively whisk together the Eggs, Half & Half, and Salt until the mixture is uniform in color and texture, is light and foamy, and there are no streaks of yolk or white.
  2. Melt the Butter in a small non-stick pan over medium heat, until the butter coats the whole pan and begins to foam.
  3. Add the Egg mixture to the center of the pan and immediately reduce the heat to medium-low.
  4. When the edges of the mixture begin to set, push the eggs from one side of the pan to the other using a rubber spatula. Continue this process, pausing between swipes to allow the uncooked egg to settle on the warm pan and cook.
  5. When the eggs are mostly cooked, but still appear wet, slowly fold the mixture onto itself a few times.
  6. When the eggs still shimmer with some moisture, remove the pan from heat and continue stirring.
  7. Transfer the mixture to serving plates. Finish with Black Pepper and Herbs to taste.
